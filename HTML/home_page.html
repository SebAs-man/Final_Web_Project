Contibuidores en Septiembre 2024

The theme for September 2024's Contributor Drop is Sky Strikers Band Together, featuring a daring themed cosmetic set based on Sky Striker Aces Raye and Roze, illustrated by hucydin on Twitter! These include the Cornered Raye and Roze avatars, Back to Back Raye and Roze sleeves, The Connecting Gates portrait, System Overload frame, and the Raye and Roze's United Front playmat. Additionally, all Contributors for the month of September will receive a 100,000 XP boost to their Battle Pass!

For those of you who don't know, Contributor Drops are a new way to reward contributors, giving them unique themed Avatars, Playmats, Sleeves, Borders, and Portraits every month. Unlike Battle Pass items, which we plan to reintroduce at a later date, Contributor Drop items are time-sensitive exclusive cosmetic items, meaning that once they're gone, they won't be available again. So be sure to become a Contributor so you won't miss out on these exclusive items!
